🧠 Publishing Rules

  • Every new page must belong to a content cluster.
  • Every new guide should link to at least three related pages when possible.
  • Every buyer's guide should use generic product categories and Product IDs.
  • Do not over-monetize informational pages.
  • Helpful content comes first; product recommendations come second.
  • Every page should have a next step for the reader.
