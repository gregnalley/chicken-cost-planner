🧱 Product Standards

  • Every product receives a unique Product ID.
  • Products are managed only inside products.js.
  • Pages should never contain hard-coded affiliate cards.
  • Pages should insert products using:
<div data-product="PRD-001"></div>
  • Headings should remain generic whenever possible.
  • Products should solve a reader problem instead of simply promoting an item.
