Transparency is important to us. This page explains how affiliate links work, how products are selected, and our commitment to honest recommendations.

Our Commitment to You

Backyard Chicken Planner exists to help backyard chicken keepers make informed decisions. While some pages may include affiliate links to products and equipment, our primary goal is to provide accurate information, practical planning tools, and useful educational resources.

We believe that trust is earned. Any commissions we receive help support the continued development of free calculators, comparison guides, and educational content that everyone can use.

Whether you purchase through one of our affiliate links or not, our mission remains the same: to provide practical information that helps you build a healthy, successful backyard flock.

What Are Affiliate Links?

Some links on Backyard Chicken Planner may be affiliate links. If you click one of these links and make a purchase, we may earn a small commission from the retailer.

There is no additional cost to you. In most cases, the price you pay is exactly the same whether you use one of our affiliate links or go directly to the retailer.

Affiliate commissions help offset the costs of operating this website, allowing us to continue creating free calculators, comparison guides, and planning resources.

How We Choose Products

Our goal is not to recommend the most expensive product or the product that pays the highest commission.

Instead, we look for products that provide genuine value to backyard chicken keepers based on factors such as:

  • Overall quality and durability
  • Ease of use
  • Value for the price
  • Suitability for beginners
  • Practical features
  • Customer reputation and long-term reliability

Whenever possible, we will explain why a particular product is recommended so readers can make informed decisions based on their own needs.

We Do Not Sell Placement

Backyard Chicken Planner does not recommend products simply because a company pays for placement or offers a higher commission.

If a product is mentioned, compared, or recommended, our goal is to explain why it may be useful for backyard chicken keepers. We may earn a commission from some links, but that does not control the educational purpose of the content.
