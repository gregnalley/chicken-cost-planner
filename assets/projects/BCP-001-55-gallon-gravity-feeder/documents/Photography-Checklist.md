# Photography Tips

□ Wipe camera lens before starting.

□ Take both landscape and portrait photos whenever possible.

□ Take more photos than you think you'll need.

□ Check focus before moving to the next step.

□ Take close-ups and wide shots.

□ Photograph mistakes as well as successes.

□ Use natural lighting whenever possible.

□ Keep the background uncluttered.
