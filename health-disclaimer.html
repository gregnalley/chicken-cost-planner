⚠️ Important Health Notice

The information in this guide is provided for educational purposes only. Backyard Chicken Planner is not a veterinary service and cannot diagnose illness or recommend treatment for your specific flock.

If your chick or chicken is severely ill, has trouble breathing, cannot stand, has significant bleeding, has bloody droppings, is rapidly declining, or multiple birds become sick at once, contact a licensed veterinarian, your local Cooperative Extension office, hatchery, or an experienced poultry professional as soon as possible.
