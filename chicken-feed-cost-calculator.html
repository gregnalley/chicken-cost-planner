🌾 How Much Does Chicken Feed Cost Per Month?

Feed is one of the biggest recurring expenses of keeping backyard chickens. Your actual cost depends on flock size, feed price, bag weight, breed type, season, waste, and whether your chickens are confined or able to forage.

    💡 Backyard Chicken Planner Tip

    Feed waste can quietly become one of the biggest hidden costs of raising chickens. A better feeder, secure feed storage, and dry feeding area can lower monthly costs without reducing the quality of care.

    ⚠️ Common Feed Cost Mistakes

    1. Only calculating feed for perfect conditions. Real-world waste, rain, and rodents can increase costs.
    2. Using the wrong feed type. Chicks, growers, layers, and mixed flocks may need different feed.
    3. Ignoring seasonal changes. Birds may eat more in cold weather and drink more in hot weather.
    4. Buying cheap feeders that waste feed. Feed waste can erase savings from cheaper equipment.
    5. Forgetting storage. Feed should stay dry and protected from rodents.
