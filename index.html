Why Use Backyard Chicken Planner?

Backyard Chicken Planner was created to make raising chickens easier, less confusing, and more affordable. Instead of guessing how many chickens to get, how much feed will cost, how large your coop should be, what supplies matter, or how to care for baby chicks, you can use our calculators and guides to build a more realistic plan.

Our goal is to help beginners avoid expensive mistakes while giving experienced chicken keepers practical tools for improving their setup.
