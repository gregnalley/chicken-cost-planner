❓ Frequently Asked Questions

How much does it cost to feed backyard chickens?

Most backyard chickens cost between $10 and $30 per month in feed depending on feed prices, breed, and feeding conditions.

How many chickens should a beginner start with?

A good starting point is 4 to 6 chickens for manageable care and steady egg production.

What is the biggest cost of raising chickens?

Feed is typically the largest ongoing expense, followed by coop and fencing setup.
