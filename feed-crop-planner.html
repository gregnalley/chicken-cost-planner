Supplemental feed, not a complete ration

Homegrown crops can provide enrichment, fresh greens, seasonal produce, forage, seeds, or stored ingredients. They should be used as supplements to an age-appropriate complete poultry feed unless a qualified poultry nutrition professional has formulated a balanced ration.
